Gentle

Gentle movement linked to breath with a restful ending

This class will introduce gentle movement and combine it with breathwork and relaxation techniques. Props like bolsters, blankets, and blocks will be utilized to facilitate ease in each of the postures. Much of the class will be done on the floor. This class is suitable for practitioners looking for a more meditative experience, those who are new to yoga, or students recovering from an injury.

Intro

A class designed just for the brand new beginner.

A class designed just for the brand new beginner. In this 45 minute introductory class, students will learn the most foundational yoga postures. Individual attention will be given to ensure proper, safe alignment. The perfect way to begin to build a practice and prepare for our more rigorous classes (if you so choose!) Absolutely no yoga experience is required nor expected.

Basic

For students of all levels of ability

This class will lay the groundwork for a successful asana practice. It will move at a slower pace to allow students to experience and integrate correct alignment in the postures. Expect to learn poses that will build strength and stamina (basic doesn't necessarily mean easy!) increase flexibility, and boost confidence in class. This class is suitable for beginning students or anyone looking to go back to the basics.

Mixed Level

Begin to explore deeper and more complex postures

This class is suitable for students of all levels of ability. Modifications will be offered to those with limitations or injuries, and advanced variations of postures will be introduced to seasoned practitioners. Expect a more vigorous class with plenty of time for pranayama (breathwork) and meditation.

Intermediate

Begin to explore deeper and more complex postures

In this class, students will begin to explore deeper and more complex variations of the physical postures. Expect to learn to balance on your head and hands, and move toward full backbends. Modifications will be offered, but this class is for students with some yoga experience.

Blackbird Bliss

Our signature class. A 90 minute immersive yoga experience.

Our signature class. A 90 minute immersive yoga experience. Expect a sweaty, dynamic flow set to music to unlock the body and clear the blocks that keep us from moving, breathing and living as we should. This will be followed by a restorative posture that calms and supports the body and allows it to drop into deep stillness. Practice will be sealed with a closing meditation. You will leave feeling clear, centered, and with a fresh outlook on who you are and how you navigate this life. A Mixed Level/Intermediate practice.
