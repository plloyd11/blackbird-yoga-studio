Gentle

Gentle movement linked to breath with a restful ending

This class will introduce gentle movement and combine it with breathwork and relaxation techniques. Props like bolsters, blankets, and blocks will be utilized to facilitate ease in each of the postures. Much of the class will be done on the floor. This class is suitable for practitioners looking for a more meditative experience, those who are new to yoga, or students recovering from an injury.

Basic

Lay the groundwork for a successful asana practice

This class will lay the groundwork for a successful asana practice. It will move at a slower pace to allow students to experience and integrate correct alignment in the postures. Expect to learn poses that will build strength, increase flexibility, and boost confidence in class. This class is suitable for beginning students or anyone looking to go back to the basics.

Mixed Level

For students of all levels of ability

This class is suitable for students of all levels of ability. Modifications will be offered to those with limitations or injuries, and advanced variations of postures will be introduced to seasoned practitioners. Expect a more vigorous class with plenty of time for pranayama (breathwork) and meditation.

Intermediate

Begin to explore deeper and more complex postures

In this class, students will begin to explore deeper and more complex variations of the physical postures. Expect to learn to balance on your head and hands, and move toward full backbends. Modifications will be offered, but this class is for students with some yoga experience.

Intermediate / Advanced

For the seasoned practitioner

This is our most rigorous class. Students will learn deep variations of the postures and fully explore the subtle aspects of yoga. Students in this class should feel comfortable practicing inversions at the wall and performing full backbends. This is a class for students with an established yoga practice.

Restorative

Allow the body to surrender to its natural relaxation response

Achieve physical, mental and emotional release. Students will experience gentle postures supported by props like blankets, bolsters, and blocks. Expect to hold the poses for a minimum of 5 minutes to allow the body to surrender to its natural relaxation response. Practitioners of all levels are welcome.
