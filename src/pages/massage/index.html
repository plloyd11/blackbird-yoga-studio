Massage Services

Your body is in good hands

Therapeutic touch and customized bodywork have endless benefits, from decreased stress and anxiety to improved range of motion and decreased pain. Massage is the perfect complement to any yoga practice. With flexible appointment times and a range of massage packages, Blackbird makes it easy to fit self-care into your schedule.

Our newly renovated massage room provides a dedicated space for massage therapy in a serene, cozy oasis.

All of our massage therapists have experience in a wide array of settings and techniques, and they are fully licensed by the state of New Jersey. Our therapists offer 60- and 90-minute massage sessions on most weekdays and weekends, as well as by special appointment.

Soothe

Soothe your mind and body. This feel-good massage combines elements of Swedish technique and aromatherapy for full-body relaxation. Think long, slow strokes, pure lavender essential oil and a warm eye pillow. Leave your worries at the door and practice true self care.

Melt

Melt your stress away. This deeper massage combines targeted bodywork for your trouble areas with delicious add-ons like hot stones and weighted blankets. Think medium-strong pressure and warm, grounding oils. Let yourself root down into pure bliss.

Unravel

Unravel all of your body’s knots. This therapeutic massage combines elements of deep tissue and targeted trigger point work to hone in on your problem areas. Think deeper, sustained pressure and healing muscle butters to dissolve tension and leave you feeling whole again.

Brandi O’Rourke

Brandi is a Licensed Massage Therapist and Reiki Master with over 13 years’ experience in high end spas, sports rehabilitation facilities and physical therapy clinics. She graduated from Mount Royal University in Calgary Alberta Canada with a degree in Massage Therapy and Bodywork. She also attended Wild Rose College of Natural Healing focusing her studies in the areas of Reiki, Chakra Balancing and Crystal Therapy. This where she gained a deep understanding of soft tissue manipulation, sports related injury rehabilitation and the body’s energy system. Most recently, Brandi has been certified in Ashiatsu (barefoot massage utilizing the therapist’s feet and body weight) by DeepFeet Bar Therapy.

After working locally in the City of Calgary, Brandi took the opportunity to travel the world while working as a massage therapist aboard Norwegian Cruise Lines. During this time, she visited over 23 countries. Upon her return, Brandi accepted a position working alongside Physical Therapists and Chiropractors at a Rehabilitation center, providing therapeutic massage and specializing in deep tissue, myofascial release, pre and post-natal and trigger-point massage. Brandi’s active lifestyle enables her to understand the needs of clients practicing yoga and how to keep them healthy and pain-free.
