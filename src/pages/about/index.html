Who We Are

The Blackbird Experience

At Blackbird Yoga Studio, we help women of every shape and size tame anxiety, release tension and find joy in their bodies so they can banish stress, age gracefully and delight in movement once and for all. We strive to create a space that invites you to experience stillness exactly as you are.

We offer a broad range of classes to suit clients of every fitness level and every movement desire. We recognize that some days ask for stillness and others call for a bit of heat. Our yoga is personal. We know your name, we know your goals, and we hold you accountable for reaching them. Our yoga is smart.
